Why Education is important

At Rainforest Trust, we help to purchase and protect the world's most threatened tropical forests and habitat for endangered species through community engagement and local partnerships. We identify key sites that target the last populations of endangered wildlife and move swiftly to save the forests, habitats and ecosystems that need it most.

On average, the world loses 100 acres of rainforest every minute. That's lush tropical forests being destroyed from logging, gold mining and agriculture. The good news is that Rainforest Trust works with some of the most highly effective and respected local communities and NGOs that protect these threatened lands and critical habitat. Rainforest Trust partners with these organizations to ensure they have the resources they need to purchase and protect these lands, ensuring their preservation.

Simultaneously, we empower local partners and indigenous communities to steward their own lands so they can independently own and manage purchased acres. With support from Rainforest Trust for education, training, job creation, and eco-tourism, we balance environmental and economic programs for a win-win solution.
